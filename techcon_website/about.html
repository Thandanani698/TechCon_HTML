History

TechCon was founded in 2010 with the vision of bringing together the brightest minds in technology. Over the years, it has grown into a global event showcasing cutting-edge innovations and industry trends.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower the tech community by fostering innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing. TechCon serves as a platform for ideas that shape the future.

Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted numerous tech luminaries over the years. Here are some of our most notable speakers:

  • Ada Lovelace: Renowned for her work on Charles Babbage's early mechanical computer.

  • Alan Turing: Known as the father of modern computing and artificial intelligence.

  • Grace Hopper: Inventor of COBOL and a pioneer in programming languages.
